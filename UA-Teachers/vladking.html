Sekijiro Kan

Sekijiro Kan, also known as Blood Hero: Vlad King, is a Pro Hero and the homeroom teacher of Class 1-B at UA High School. Though he comes across as gruff and strict, he deeply cares for his students, willing to go out of his way to protect them and encourage them to do their absolute best.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a dark red skin-tight suit with a very low U-shaped collar, white over-the-knee boots, and thick vambraces over his lower arms. He also wears a three-piece metallic belt and an orange mask over his eyes.

Quirk

Kan's Quirk, Blood Control, allows him to freely manipulate his own blood after it has left his body. He can also solidify the ejected blood, allowing him to trap his opponents.

Fighting Style

Kan's fighting style consists of Melee Combat. He is much stronger than the average human and is able to react quickly in tense situations.
