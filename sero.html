Hanta Sero

Hanta Sero, also known as the Taping Hero: Cellophane, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Hanta has a tall and lean figure. He has black, chin-length, spiky hair, black eyes and a straight teeth that dominate his grin. His elbows have the shape of cylindrical tape dispensers. Whe going to U.A., he wears the offical U.A. uniform.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of a plain black, skin-tight bodysuit with a turtleneck, which has a white design on his mid-torso. He also has white boots, white short bands around his waist, the sides of his thighs and larger ones across his chest, shoulder pads that are shaped like tape dispensers and a yellow helmet with a large black visor obscuring his face.

Quirk

Hanta's Quirk is called Tape. He has tape dispenser-like organs in his elbows, from which he can shoot long cellophane tape-like strips, that seem to be sturdier than regular tape. He can use his tape to wrap people, swing long distances and create traps.

A drawback to his Quirk is that his skin can dry out when he overuses it. Through intense training, he increases his capacity and improves his tape's textile strength ans shooting speed.

Further Information

Hanta was born in Tokyo. He has a very friendly and sociable personality, as he can make friends with those around him easily.

He loves to eat oranges, soy products and anything else he might find healthy.
