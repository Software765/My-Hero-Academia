Shihai Kuroiro

Shihai Kuroiro, also known as The Scheming Hero: Vantablack, is a student in Class 1-B at UA High School. He is a sly and deceitful schemer, who prefers to catch people off guard.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a simple black jumpsuit with light shoes and a large wristband on his left arm with three rectangular indentations.

Quirk

Kuroiro's Quirk, Black, allows him to merge his body into anything that is dark in colour, whether naturally dark or dark via external circumstances, such as shadows.

Through intense training, he can now move said black things upon possession, although this is limited to entites that are normally capable of moving.

Fighting Style

Kuroiro's fighting style consists of Melee and Support Combat. He uese his Quirk to hide from enemy attacks or to execute ambushes. Additionally, once merged with a black-coloured object, he can move through its whole structure at very high speeds, even while carrying a person.

Kuroiro's known UA Data
