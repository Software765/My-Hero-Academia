Shota Aizawa

Shota Aizawa, also known as the Erasure Hero: Eraser Head, is a Pro Hero and the homeroom teacher of U.A.'s Class 1-A.

Appearance

Shota has a slender and tall figure. He has messy, shoulder-length black hair that partially hangs in front of his face and half-opened black eyes. His facial hair remains unkempt anf after being heavily injured by Nomu, he has a deep scar underneath his right eye.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of a long-sleeved, black shirt and matching trousers that is tuck into his black boots. He also wears a utility belt and his signature wrap scarf. He hides a pair of yellow goggles underneath his scarf.

Quirk

Shota's Quirk is called Erasure. It gives him the ability to nullify another person's Quirk by looking at the user. This Quirk, however, has no offensive potential.

There are many drawbacks to this Quirk - It deactivates if Shota blinks or his line of sight is blocked, it gives him bad dry eye which forces him to blink more often when he uses the power continuously, Mutant-Class Quirks are immune to it and the Quirk causes his hair to stand on end, which allows his enemies to see if his Quirk is active or not.

Further Information

Shota was born in Tokyo. He is shown to have a very stern and mostly reserved personality, coming across as cold, apathetic and impatient. He is known to expel students who he feels are not suited for U.A. High. However, he has not done so with Class 1-A and has shown to care for his students.

He has a fondness with cats.
