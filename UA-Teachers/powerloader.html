Higari Maijima

Higari Maijima, also known as Excavation Hero: Power Loader, is a Pro Hero and a member of the UA High School faulty, who manages the Support Course Development Studio. He is a strict teacher who desires to see his students excel without contraint.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a large yellow helmet in the shape of a excavator claw, large, fingerless grey gloves with metal gurads and plain cyan trousers. In addition, he doesn't wear a shirt with his costume.s

Quirk

Maijima's Quirk, Iron Claws, gives his fingers metallic claws that are good for digging/mining through the ground.

Fighting Style

Maijima's fighting style is Support.
