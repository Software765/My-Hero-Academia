Yosetsu Awase

Yosetsu Awase, also known as Welder, is a student in Class 1-B at UA High School. Although he is not as social as others, he is foul-mouthed and tense, except for life-or-death situations, where he becomes very easily startled and more talkative. Despite this, he has shown to be very selfless and willing to put himself in danger to fulfill a plan.

Hero Cosutme

Awase's hero costume consists of a burgundy, baggy, full body suit, with its collar passing his chin. He wears shoulders pads and a bag on his belt where he keeps various items, including blocks of wood and metal for his Quirk. He also switches his usual zig-zagged headband for a striped one.

Quirk

Awase's Quirk, Weld, gives him the ability to fuse objects together at an atomic. It is effective on both organic and inorganic material, although he needs to be touching both objects in order to fuse them.

Fighting Style

Awase's fighting style consists of Support Combat. He has demonstrated excellent handling of his Quirk, a power that functions better as a restraining tool, giving him a way to incapacitate enemies by harmlessly binding them to nearby structures.

Awase's known UA Data

  • Student/Seat No. 1
  • Ranked 10th in the UA Entrance Exam
  • Passed in the Provisional Licence Exam, earning his licence.
