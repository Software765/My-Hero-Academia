Chiyo Shuzenji

Chiyo Shuzenji, also known as Youthful Heroine: Recovery Girl, is a nurse at UA High School. She is kind and compassionate, treating everyone with kindness. Despite this, she does have a strict and reproaching attitude towards foolish behaviour that can lead to injuries

Hero Cosutme

Her hero costume consists of a doctor's lab coat and a dress with a yellow and red vest-like designs on either side, two yellow buttons, and a belt with a pink 'R'-shaped buckle. She also wears pink boots and a helmet around the sides of her head, a purple-tinted visor joining it over her eyes. In addition, she walks with a cane designed like a syringe that she can also use for healing the injured.

Quirk

Shuzenji's Quirk, Heal, allows her to amplify and speed up the human body's natural healling process by planting a kiss on the injured person.

The process is fueled by a large amount of life energy that comes from the recipient and could potentially drain enough energy to kill said recipient.

The main drawback to her Quirk is that it can't heal or completely heal injuries that are too severe, as well as being unable to regrow lost body parts.
