Toru Hagakure

Toru Hagakure, also known as the Stealth Hero: Invisible Girl, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A High School.

Appearance

Toru is fairly short with a completely invisible body. She is only identifiable through held or worn objects, like clothing and accessories. When clothed, her body appears to be slender yet fairly curvaceous.

Hero Costume

Her hero costume only consists of a pair of baby blue gloves with pale pink lines, and light brownish-grey lace-up shoes.

Her winter costume consists of plain knee-high boots and slightly thicker-looking gloves with a different colour scheme and design.

Quirk

Toru's Quirk is called Invisibility. Her body is completely invisible.

It is unknown if it is permanently active or can be tunred on and off at will, but she has shwon to still be invisible when asleep or unconscious. This leans to the former idea.

Further Information

Toru was born in Tokyo. She has a bubbly, cheery and easygoing personality, doing most things with an upbeat attitude.

She loves to eat caramel and watch dokkiri shows, which are hidden-camera surprise prank shows.

Status in Class 1-A

  • 4th Oldest in the Class
  • Student/Seat No. 16
  • Her rank in the U.A. Entrance Exam is unknown
  • Ranked 18th in the Quirk Apprehension Test
  • Ranked 16th in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked in the Top 42 in the U.A. Sports Festival
  • She passed in the Provisional Hero License Exam, earning her license.
