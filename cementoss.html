Ken Ishiyama

Ken Ishiyama, also known as Cementoss, is a Pro Hero and member of the U.A. High School faculty where he teaches Modern Literature.

Appearance

Ken appears to be a humanoid block of cement. He has black eyes, grey hair tied into a short ponytail and his lips are pulled upwards.

Outfit

His hero costume consists of a sleeveless brown suit that includes yellow stripes, shorts and an elongated colar. He also wears grey shoes.

Quirk

Ken's Quirk is called Cement. It allows him to manipulate any cement based material that he touches, allowing him to shape it into various forms.

This Quirk doesn't seem to have a time limit, which makes him the most difficult of the U.A. teachers to fight against.

Further Information

Ken was born in the Yamagata Prefecture. He has a serious yet pleasant personality, being very careful of the well being of others.

He likes round things and his favourite food is manju. He is also a big fan of All Might.
