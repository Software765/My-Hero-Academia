Thirteen

Space Hero: Thirteen is a Pro Hero who specialises in search and resuce, and a faculty member of U.A. High School.

Appearance

Thriteen's physical appearance is unknown. It is possible that Thriteen is then, given the quite small legs showing between the pants and shoes.

Outfit

Her hero costume consists of a white spacesuit design, using what appears to be a puffy jacket instead of an actual suit. She also wears a black helmet with white eyes, and a pair of yellow boots.

Quirk

Thriteen's Quirk is called Black Hole. It allows her to replicate a black hole suction effect from her fingertips.

It is a very powerful Quirk that not even light can escape it, as anything sucked is disintegrated into dust.

Further Information

Thriteen was born in Kagoshima Prefecture. She has a wise and well-mannered personality.

She likes museums and self-learning documentaries.
