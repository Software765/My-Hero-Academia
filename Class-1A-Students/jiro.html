Kyoka Jiro

Kyoka Jiro, also known as The Hearing Hero: Earphone Jack, is a student in Class 1-A at UA High School. She has a pragmatic, unethusiastic, and teasing personailty that is mostly shown to thosee she finds irritating. Despite this, she has shown to be nice and friendly, very defensive towards her friends and empathetic with others' feelings.

Hero Costume

First Design

Her hero costume consists of a black leather jacket, long salmon-coloured shirt with several rips at the collar and hem, and black pants and boots, which has stereos built into the shafts. She also has two small triangular red paint marks below her eyes, a plain black choker and white fingerless gloves.

Second Design

For the Provisional Hero License Exam, she upgrades her costume by gaining a set of headphones and two bracelets that double up as her amplifiers. Later, her facepaint changes to two larger chevron-shaped marks.

Quirk

Jiro's Quirk, Earphone Jack, gives her a pair of headphone jacks hanging from her earlobes. When plugged into something, she can channel the sound of her heartbeat into it, in the form of a violent vibration attack. She can also stretch her earlobes several meters. In addition, her Quirk allows her to hear minuscule sounds and vibrations from her surroundings. Through intense training, she increases the amount of audio she can hear.

Fighting Style

Jiro's fighting style consists of Medium-ranged and Support Combat, along with Reconnaissance. She utilises the perhensile nature of her Quirk in whip-like fashion, and use the sound generating effects of her Quirk to shock targets with vibrations, effectively stunning and sometimes defeating them. In addition, she can pllug them into the ground, and generate sound waves strong enough to make the ground quake, cause fissures, and disrupt large enemy formatioons.
