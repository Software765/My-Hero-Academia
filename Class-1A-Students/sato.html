Rikido Sato

Rikido Sato, also known as The Sweet Hero: Sugarman, is a student in Class 1-A at UA High School. He has an interest in sweets and baking, the latter being useful for his Quirk.

Hero Costume

Sato's hero costume consists of a yellow full bodysuit, covering the entirety of his body, excluding his mouth, eyes and hair. He also wears white gloves, white boots and a utility belt that carries small quantities of sugar in pouches.

Quirk

Sato's Quirk, Sugar Rush, allows him to increase his strength five-fold for three minutes. For this to happen, he needs to consume 10 grams of sugar. For every additional 10 grams consume, he extends the time of increased strength by another three minutes.

The main drawback to his Quirk is that when converting sugar into strength, his cognitive functions gradually drop, making him very sleepy afterwards. Through intenst training, he has increased the amount of augmented physical strength granted by his Quirk

Fighting Style

Sato's fighting style consists of Melee Combat. Due to his Quirk, he is a straightforward fighter who can take advantage of the temporaily augmented physical might to punch his way through obstacles and enemies.

Sato's known UA Data

  • Student/Seat No. 10
  • Ranked 12th in the Quirk Apprehension Test
  • Ranked 12th in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked in the Top 42 in the UA Sports Festival
  • Passed in the Provisional Hero License Exam, earning his license.
