Ochako Uraraka

Ochako Uraraka, also known as Uravity, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School and is one of the main protagonists in the My Hero Academia anime.

Appearance

Ochako is short with a slender yet feminine build. She has shoulder-length brown hair in the style of a bob with two long bangs on the sides of her face. She also has big, round brown eyes and permentant blushes on each cheek. When entering U.A., she wear the standard female U.A. uniform, only instead of wearing black, knee-high socks, she wears black tights.

Hero Costume

Her hero costume consists of a mostly black with pale pink bodysuit, pink circular wrist guards, knee-high pink boots and a two-piece belt, connected with a circle piece around her waist. She also wears a pink helmet with a tinted visor. The accessories are desgined to press her acupuncture points.

Quirk

Ochako's Quirk is called Zero Gravity. It allows her to nullify the effects of gravity on things by touching them with the pads on her fingertips, causing them to become weightless. She can also cancel this effect by touching her fingertips together.

A drawback to her Quirk is that she can suffer from severe nausea when she execeeds her weight limit. This can also happen when she makes herself float for a long time. Through intense training, she can float for a short period without falling sick. She has also increased her weight limit through training as well.

Further Information

Ochako was born in the Mie Prefecture and lived with her mother and father. She comes from a poor household, which makes her become excited over expensive things and for her to save money. This also gives her drive into becoming a hero, wantng to earn money for her parents, so they can live a easier life.

She has a very warm, lively personality, who thinks about everything positively. Because of this, she has made friends with many of her classmates.

She loves the starry sky and she loves to eat Japanese food, espeically mochi.

Status in Class 1-A

  • 5th Youngest in the Class
  • Student/Seat No. 5
  • Ranked 3rd place in the U.A. Entrance Exam
  • Ranked 10th in the Quirk Apprehension Test
  • Ranked 13th in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked Top 16 of the U.A. Sports Festival
  • She passed in the Provisional Hero License Exam, earning her license.
