Mashirao Ojiro

Mashirao Ojiro, also known as The Martial Arts Hero: Tailman, is a student in Class 1-A at UA High School. He has a very calm and noble personality, with a deep sense of dignity. He also described as a hard worker. On the other hand, he can be sheepish and bashful. Overall, he is reliable focused during hero work.

Hero Cosutme

First Design

Ojiro's hero costume consists of a plain karate gi, tied with a black belt and its collar partially lined with fur. He also wears black boots with sand blue details.

Second Design

Before the Provisional License Exam, he slightly upgrades his costume by adding sand blue fingerless gloves.

Winter Costume

His winter costume simply consists of a large coat with a thicker fur collar and a black shirt underneath his gi.

Quirk

Ojiro's Quirk, Tail, gives him a long, powerful, strudy tail. It effectively functions as a third arm that he can use to grab things, and long and strong enough to break objects.

He is incredibly adept at using his tail, using it for close-range combat and to increase his speed and mobility.

Through intense training, he has increased the strength and durability of his tail.

Fighting Style

Ojiro's fighting style consists of Melee Combat. He uses his Quirk to either deliver direct attacks or assit his movements in battle. His fighting style invloves combining his perhensile tail with his knowledge of martial arts to engage enemies in close-range combat, performing fast and acrobatic techniques.

Ojiro's known UA Data

  • Ranked 8th in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked in the Top 42 in the UA Sports Festival
