Ibara Shiozaki

Ibara Shiozaki, also known as Vine, is a student in Class 1-B at UA High School. She is a very modest and courteous student who values honesty above all else. Despite her graceful and soft-spoken personality, she is not afraid to make her pure intentions known and has shown to have an unexpected vindictive side.

Hero Cosutme

Her hero costume consists of a plain white robe and black knee-high boots.

Quirk

Shiozaki's Quirk, Vines, gives her hair-like vines that grow on her head. She can lengthen and manipulate the vines, allowing her to attack, defend and grapple. She can also detach from them at will.

The vines will grow back, provided her heand receives proper sunlight and water.

Fighting Style

Shiozaki's fighting style consists of Medium-range Combat. She seems to be the stationary-type combatant, relying on the restricting properties of her vines in battles while she stands still.

Shiozaki's known UA Data

  • Student/Seat No. 8
  • Ranked 4th in the UA Entrance Exam
  • Ranked Top 8 in the UA Sports Festival
  • Passed in the Provisional Licence Exam, earning her licence
