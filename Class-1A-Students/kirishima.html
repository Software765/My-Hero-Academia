Eijiro Kirishima

Eijiro Kirishima, also known as The Sturdy Hero: Red Riot, is a student in Class 1-A at UA High School. He has a boisterous and outgoing personailty with a fondness of the concept of manliness. In addition, he is honest and kind-hearted, following a specific code of chivalry the motivates him to live without regret, inspired by the pro hero Crimson Riot.

Hero Costune

First Design

Kirishima's hero costume consists of two dark red, gear-shaped shoulder pads, and a jagged sash joining from his left pad to the right of his belt, which has the letter 'R' in the centre. He also wears baggy black trousers with a half cape and dark red boots. He leaves his chest bare and wears a wired face guard.

Second Design

His costume gets an upgrade for the Provisional Hero License Exam. He now wears black sleeves to prevent him hurting civilains with his Quirk during rescues.

Quirk

Kirishima's Quirk, Hardening, gives him the power to harden and sharpen any or all parts of his body, causing his flesh to become jagged and rock-like.

His Quirk grants him greatly enhanced durability and can withstand a great deal of punishment for extended periods of time.

The main drawback to his Quirk is that there is a limit to how much damage his hardened skin can withstand, causing it to slowly dissipate and make him lose stamina. Through intense training, he is able to increase his strength and durability.

Fighting Style

Kirishima's fighting style consists of Melee Combat. His style is straightforward and reckless, letting his hardened body take opposing hits while delivering counterattacks. With his Quirk, he is able to turn himself into a human shield for allies or civilians.
