Toshinori Yagi

Toshinori Yagi, more commonly known by All Might, is the former No.1 Hero who bore the title of the world's Symbol of Peace. He also teaches Foundational Hero Studies at U.A. High School. He is the tritagonist of the My Hero Academia anime.

Appearance

In his empowered form, Toshinori is very tall with an equally-muscular and well-defined physique, resembling a typical western comic book superhero. He has short blonde hair with two distinct tufts that sticks up above his head. He has dark shadow over his face that hides everything but his bright blue eyes.

In his true form, his declining health, after sustaining permanent damage for All for One, has reduce him to a very skinny appearance with sharp, angular features, long limps, long neck and eyebrows absent. He has alarge scar that covers most of his left side.

Outfit

His 'Golder Age' hero costume consisted of a skin-tight blue bodysuit, decorated with a red symbol, that somewhat resembles a 'Y'. He also wears a gold belt that has a silver buckle with red eyes and shin-length gold boots. His forearm sleeves were coloured in gold, with spiked fins on the sides of each arm.

Quirk

Toshinori's Quirk is called One for All. It is an unique quirk that has been passed down for generations. It allows him to stockpile massive amount of power, enhancing their physical abilities. He was Quirkless before Nana Shimura gave it to him, making him the Eigth Holder of One for All.

When he gave the Quirk to Izuku Midoriya, the power in him decrease gradually. Soon, he used up all of its embers to defeat All for One, forcing him to retire.

Further Information

Toshinori was born in Tokyo. His hero persona has a bright and colourful personality, always sporting a giant smile that is said to inspire others as well as help them feel safe and hopeful. When he reverts to his true form, however, he becomes less energetic and more serious. Two personality traits that seem to be alway present are his optimism and oafishness.

He loves watching movies and Yakushima cedars (anicent tree).
