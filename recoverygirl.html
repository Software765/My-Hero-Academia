Chiyo Shuzenji

Chiyo Shuzenji, also known as Youthful Herorine 'Recovery Girl' is a nurse at U.A. High School, housing two offices at the school and the Sports Festival Stadium.

Appearance

Chiyo is short and elderly. She has grey hair styled in a netted bun, with a large syringe poking diagonally out from the left side of it. She also has a small eyes and a long mouth with defined nasolabial folds.

Outfit

She wears a doctor's lab coat and a dress with a yellow and red vset-like design, two yellow buttons and a belt with a 'R' shaped buckle. She also wears pink boots and a helmet around the sides of her head, with a purple tinted visor. She walks with a cane designed like a syringe.

Quirk

Chiyo's Quirk is called Heal. It allows her to amplify and spped up the human body's natural healing process by planting a kiss on the injured person. This is fueled by a large amount of life energy that comes from the recipient.

The drawbacks to her Quirk is that it could potentialyly drain enough energy to kill the recipient and it's not able to regrow lost body parts.

Further Information

Chiyo was born in Tokyo. She has a very kind and compassionate personality to everyone, although she can be strict and reproaching towards those that have done something foolish, namely All Might.
