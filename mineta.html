Minoru Mineta

Minoru Mienta, also known as the Fresh-Picked Hero: Grape Juice, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Minoru has a very small build with a large, round head. He has oval-shaped black eyes and four large purple and black balls that resembles a mohawk. When attending U.A., he wears the standard male U.A. uniform.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of a purple shirt and mask with a yellow cape, yellow boots and yellow gloves. He also wears white pants that stick out quite a lot.

Quirk

Minoru's Quirk is called Pop Off. It allows him to produce and pluck ball-shpaed objects from his head. These are incredibly sticky and will stick onto any surface.

A drawback to his Quirk is that if he plucks too many, it will cause him to start bleeding from his head.

Further Information

Minoru was born in the Kanagawa Prefecture. He has a timid but very perverted personality that makes him unpopular with the girls and even some of the boys. His interest in women in one of his motivations in becoming a hero, as well as popularity.

He loves girls a lot.
