Fumikage Tokoyami

Fumikage Tokoyami, also known as the Jet-Black Hero: Tsukuyomi, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Fumikage has a short build. He has the head of a black bird, resembling a crow or a raven, with a yellow beak and red eyes, and wears a red choker. Despite the bird head, his body is of a normal human. When going to U.A., he wears the offical U.A. uniform.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of a black robe, but tinited a dark purple where light hits it, that covers his entire body, only stopping halfway down his shins. He also wears knee-high black boots.

Quirk

Fumikage's Quirk is called Dark Shadow. It allows him to mainfest a sentient shadow monster from his body that he can materialise and contract freely.

A drawback to his Quirk is the personality, strength, defense and control of the shadow depends on light - in bright light, it is weaker and smaller, but easier to control. In total darkness, it is much bigger and stronger, but diffcult to control. Through intense training, Fumikage increases his control over Dark Shadow, even when in the dark.

Further Information

Fumikage was born in the Shizuoka Prefecture. He has a reserved, serious and focused personality, albeit noble and valiant as well. He has a fondness witht the darkness and othe related concepts.

He loves gloomy places and likes to eact apples.

Status in Class 1-A

  • 6th Youngest in the Class
  • Student/Seat No. 14
  • Ranked 9th in the U.A. Entrance Exam
  • Ranked 5th in the Quirk Apprehension Test
  • Ranked 14th in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked 3rd in the U.A. Sports Festival
  • He passed in the Provisional Hero License Exam, earning him his license.
