Snipe

Snipe is a Pro Hero and a member of the teaching staff at UA High School, in charge of the third year students. He is levelheaded and rational, showing to be quite intelligent and knowledgeable.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a plain black tank top with white armour under a large red cloak, buttoned up at the top, a brown belt with a gun holster, and baggy black trousers with light brown chaps. He also wears black skin tight arm-guards, tan gloves, and grey boots with a spur attached to the back. In addition, he wears a brown cowboy hat with a large 'S' in front, and his face is covered by an old-school tan gas mask.

Quirk

Snipe's Quirk, Honing, allows him to lock on to any target he can see within six hundred meters and make any projectile he releases chase after said target.

The main drawback with his Quirk is that he can't choose what part of the target's body his projectile hits and his attacks don't carry much strength.

Fighting Style

Snipe's fighting style consists of Ranged Combat.
