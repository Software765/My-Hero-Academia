Lunch Rush

Cook Hero: Lunch Rush is a Pro Hero and the chef at the cafeteria of UA High School. He has an upbeat attitude, always giving a thumbs-up as a form of reply.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a cooking apron and a chef hat, and a mask that covers his face. He also wears white gloves and shoes. In addition, he wears a tube that connects from his mouth to his back.

Quirk

Lunch Rush's Quirk is unknown, but it seems to be linked with food and cooking.

Fighting Style

Lunch Rush's fighting style consists of Support.
