Sen Kaibara

Sen Kaibara, also known as Spiral, is a student in Class 1-B at UA High School. He is a level-headed student who remains mostly calm and focused on many occasion. But despite this demeanor, he can sometimes warm up.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a dark-coloured windbreaker with grey, drill patterned rings around the base of the neck and arms. In addition, he wears a large white trench coat and gloves.

Quirk

Kaibara's Quirk, Gyrate, allows him to make any part of his body rotate at high speeds like a drill.

Fighting Style

Kaibara's fighting style consists of Melee Combat. By using the gyrating effects of his Quirk in tandem with his hand-to-hand combat abilites, he can deliver powerful spinning attacks that can damage his enemies.
