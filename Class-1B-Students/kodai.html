Yui Kodai

Yui Kodai, also known as Rule, is a student in Class 1-B at UA High School. She is a quiet student that rarely expresses any emotions. Despite her silent demeanor, she isn't moody and has shown to care for her classmates.

Hero Cosutme

Her hero costume consists of a simple red bodysuit with a white-collar trim, a matching 'V' pattern over her chest and around her upper arms, which also includes a short white skirt. She also wears plain vambraces, a belt with a small pouch, and a red hat which resembles the front of a baseball cap and a metal fin at the back.

Quirk

Kodai's Quirk, Size, allows her to alter the size of any non-living object that she touches. She can return objects to their original size by pressing her fingertips together.

Fighting Style

Kodai's fighting style consists of Long-range Combat.
