Juzo Honenuki

Juzo Honenuki, also known as Mudman, is a student in Class 1-B at UA High School. He is shown to be a calm and understanding student, willing to draw out of battles in unfavourable condition. Although, he has express his dislike towards losing.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a dark bodysuit with a set of orange armour over the top and a mask that covers his face. The armour comprises of a three-piece chest plate, vambraces and plates over his thighs, calves and upper arms. He also wears a pair of elevated, green and orange boots with pipe-like shafts on his feet, white gloves and white kneepads.

Quirk

Honenuki's Quirk, Softening, allows him to soften any non-living thing he touches. For instance, he can soften the ground to make it similar to quicksand. If he makes contact again, he can undo the effect.

Fighting Style

Honenuki's fighting style consists of Long-range Combat. He can use his Quirk effectively against opponents who rely on their footing during a battle. This includes trapping enemies in the ground, hindering movements and mobility.
