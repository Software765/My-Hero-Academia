Koji Koda

Koji Koda, also known as The Petting Hero: Anima, is a student in Class 1-A at UA High School. Despite his brutish appearance, he has a very shy and quiet personality, rarely speaking and preferring to use sign language to communicate with his classmates.

Hero Costume

First Design

Koda's hero costume consists of a tight yellow suit, reaching his knees and elbows, with a large red marking over his torso and yellow shoes. On his chest, there's a symbol resembling an open mouth.

Second Design

His costume gets an upgrade in preparation for the Provisional Hero License Exam. He adds two white straps over his shouders, wears a mask that covers his entire face, with holes over his mouth, and changed his shoes into boots.

Quirk

Koda's Quirk, Anivoice, gives him the ability to command any creature in the aniaml kingdom by speaking to them.

His Quirk is shown to be effective in creating a smokescreen with a flock of birds, along with attacking an opponent. He also uses a swarm of insects to distract and attack opponents.

The main drawback to his Quirk is that he is unable to exert his control if the animal cannot hear his command. Also, the target must be a real animal and not merely animal-like. Through intense training, he has increased the range of his Anivoice, allowing him to control animals from long distances.

Fighting Style

Koda's fighting style consists of Melee and Support Combat. He is able to use a variety of animals for different situations, like using bugs for sneak attacks and birds for reconnaissance.

Koda's known UA Data

  • Student/Seat No. 9
  • Ranked 11th in the Quirk Apprehension Test
  • Ranked 11th in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked in the Top 42 in the UA Sports Festival
  • Passed in the Provisional Hero License Exam, earning his license.
