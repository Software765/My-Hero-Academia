Ruo Inui

Ryo Inui, also known as the Hunting Dog Hero: Hound Dog, is a Pro Hero and the Lifestyle Guidance Counselor at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Ryo has the appearance of a dog. He has scuffy blonde hair sticking out and wears a muzzle over his snout.

Quirk

Ryo's Quirk is called Dog. It gives him the attributes and abilities of a dog, such as a heightened sense of smell.

Further Information

Ryo was born in Tokyo. He has an incoherent and violet personality when he is angry, having a mix of words and a sound akin to a dog's growling.
