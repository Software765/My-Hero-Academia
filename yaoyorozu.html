Momo Yaoyorozu

Momo Yaoyorozu, also known as the Everything Hero: Creati, is Vice President of Class 1-A at U.A. High School, where she got in through offical recommendations.

Appearance

Momo is fairly tall with a rather mature physique. She has long black hair tied in a spiky ponytail and onyx eyes. When attendinh U.A., she wears the offical U.A. uniform.

Hero Costume

Her hero costume consists of a high-collared, sleeveless crimson leotard, which is open to expose her skin from her neck to just below her navel. She also wears calf-length crimson boots with heels and two gold utility belts around her waist.

For her winter costume, she wears a long, high-collared red cape, with a ring going around her neck that has a large gemstone at her throat.

Quirk

Momo's Quirk is called Creation. It gives her the ability to create any non-living material/oject from her exposed skin by transforming the molecular structure of her fat cells. She does however needs to understand the molecular structure of what the material/oject is made of before she can create it.

The more she eats, the material she has to work with, so she needs to considerable foor ingestion for her Quirk to be effective. This can cause a drawback to her Quirk, being if she creates too much without replacing her calarories, she can become weak and possibly anemic. It also takes a longer period of time and more skin exposed to create large objects.

Further Information

Momo was born in the Aichi Prefecture and lived with her mother and father. She comes from a rich background and sometimes mentions it to her classmates, though it's never out of malice.

She has a very prudent and dedicated personality, who acts as a natural leader as she is generally level-headed and calm in dangerous situations.

Momo loves to read, especially encyclopedias.
