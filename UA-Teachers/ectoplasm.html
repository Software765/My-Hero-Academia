Ectoplasm

Ectoplasm is a Pro Hero and member of the U.A. High School faculty, who teaches Mathematics.

Appearance

Ectoplasm is tall and slender. He has short hair and completely blank eyes. Due to losing his legs in the line of duty, he uses replacement prosthetics.

Outfit

His hero costume consists of a tan cloak that covers most of his body. Underneath it, he wears a skin-tight black bodysuit with a golden striped neck area and a matching black helmet that covers his entire head. He also wears baggy dark grey trousers and a pair of thin, lightweight prosthetic legs.

Quirk

Ectoplasm's Quirk is called Clones. It gives him the ability to produce streams of ectoplasm from his mouth and transform it into clones of himself - up to 30 clones.

Further Information

Ectoplasm was born in the Okinawa Prefecture. He has a steadfast and unrelenting personality, which has made him highly popular as a hero.

He loves karaoke.
