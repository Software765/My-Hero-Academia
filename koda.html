Koji Koda

Koji Koda, also known as the Petting Hero: Anima, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Koji has a tall and muscular build, with peach-coloured skin and black eyes. His head takes the form of a rock, which is unevenly shaped and pointed at the top. When attending U.A., he wears the standard male U.A. uniform.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of a tight yellow suit, reaching his knees and elbows, with a large red marking over his torso and yellow shoes. On his chest, there's a symbol resembling an open mouth.

For the Provisional Hero License Exam, he upgardes to include two white straps over his shoulders and a mask the covers his entire face with holes over his mouth. He also swaps his shoes for boots instead.

Quirk

Koji's Quirk is called Anivoice. It gives him the ability to command any creature in the aniaml kingdom by speaking to them.

A drawback to his Quirk is that he is unable to exert his control if the animal cannot hear his command. Also, the target must be a real animal and not merely animal-like.

Further Information

Koji was born in the Iwate Prefecture and lived with his mother. Despite his appearance, he has a very shy and quiet personality, rarely speaking and preferring to use sign language to communicate with his classmates.

He loves nature.

Status in Class 1-A

  • 3rd Youngest in the Class
  • Student/Seat No. 9
  • His rank in the U.A. Entrance Exam is unknown
  • Ranked 11th in the Quirk Apprehension Test
  • Ranked 11th in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked in the Top 42 in the U.A. Sports Festival
  • He passed in the Provisional Hero License Exam, earning his license.
