Ochako Uraraka

Ochako Uraraka, also known as Uravity, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High Schol and is one of the main protagonists in the My Hero Academia anime.

Appearance

Ochako is a short girl with a slender yet feminine build. She has shoulder-length brown hair in the style of a bob with two long bangs on the sides of her face. She also has big, round brown eyes and permentant blushes on each cheek. When at U.A., she wear the offical uniform, with the addition of black tights.

Hero Costume

Her hero costume consists of a mostly black with pale pink bodysuit, pink circular wrist guards, knee-high pink boots and a two-piece belt, connected with a circle piece around her waist. She also wear a pink helmet with a tinted visor. The accessories are desgined to press her acupuncture points.

Quirk

Ochako's Quirk is called Zero Gravity. It allows her to nullify the effects of gravity on things by touching them with the pads on her fingertips, causing them to become weightless. She can also cancel this effect by toching her fingertips together and saying "Release".

A drawback to her quirk is that she can suffer from severe nausea when she execeeds her weight limit. This can also happen when she makes herself float for a long time. Thanks to training at U.A. though, she can float for a short period without falling sick. She has also increased her weight limit through training as well.

Further Information

Ochako was born in the Mie Prefecture and lived with her mother and father. She comes from a poor household, which makes her become excited over expensive things and for her to save money. This also gives her drive into becoming a hero, wantng to earn money for her parents, so they can live a easier life.

She has a very warm, lively individual who thinks about everything positively. Because of this, she made friends with many of her classmates, especially Izuku Midoriya, Tsuyu Asui and Tenya Iida. She's especially close with Izuku, even developing a crush on him, which she pushes asides to focus on her hero training.

Her favourite thing is the starry sky and she loves Japanese food, espeically mochi.
