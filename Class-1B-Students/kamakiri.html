Togaru Kamakiri

Toharu Kamakiri, also known as Jack Mantis, is a student in Class 1-B at UA High School. He is a hot-blooded student who appears rather aggressive in terms of behaviour.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a large dark green cloak, plain black trousers and three-toed boots. He also wears a mask around his eyes and has a zig-zag pattern on his shirt.

Quirk

Kamakiri's Quirk, Razor Sharp, allows him to produce large blades from any parts of his body. These blades are sharp and strong enough to cut through pipes made of steel and iron, and durable enough to withstand a point-blank explosion.

Fighting Style

Kamakiri's fighting style consists of Close Range Combat.

Kamakiri's known UA Data

  • Student/Seat No. 3
  • Passed in the Provisional Licence Exam, earning his licence.
