Manga Fukidashi

Manga Fukidashi, also known as Comicman, is a student in Class 1-B at UA High School. He appears to be an expressive and dramatic person. He also seems to have a soft spot for children as he enjoys making them smile.

Hero Cosutme

Fukidashi's hero costume consists of a blue shirt with red sleeves and trousers, covered by a series of ink bottle-like pieces of armour around his torso and legs, black on his torso and white on his legs. He wears a belt with mange panels, elblow under-length gloves and a mask resembling a manga page template.

Quirk

Fukidashi's Quirk, Comic, allows him to materialise words in midair by speaking them. These words are onomatopeia, which can manifest the effects of the phenomenon that the sound suggests.

The main drawback to his Quirk is that he can get an incredibly sore throat when he overuses it.

Fighting Style

Fukidashi's fighting style consists of Long-range Combat. With his power, he creates onomatopoeia that can spawn different effects, making him a versatile combatant that excels in offense, defense, and support.

Fukidashi's known UA Data
