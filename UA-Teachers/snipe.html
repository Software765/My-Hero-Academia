Ken Ishiyama

Snipe is a Pro Hero and a member of the teaching staff at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Snipe has the appearance of a stereotypical western cowboy. He has dark, purplish shoulder-length dreadlocks and his face is covered by an old-school tan gas mask, with triangular holes for his eyes and silver ear-defenders to the sides of the mask.

Outfit

He wears a plain black tank top with white armour strapped over the right side of his torso, a brown cowboy hat, grey boots and a large red cloak. He also wears a brown belt with a gin holster, baggy dark brown trousers with light brown chaps over them. On his arms, he wears black tight armguards and tan gloves.

Quirk

Snipe's Quirk is called Homing. It allows him to control the trajectory of any and all of the bullets he shoots.

Further Information

Snipe was born in the Kanagawa Prefecture. His mannerisms are similar to that of a traditional cowboy. He appears very knowledgeable and always has something intelligent to say.
