Ken Ishiyama

Ken Ishiyama, also known as Cementoss, is a Pro Hero and member of the UA High School faculty where he teaches Modern Literature. He is often serious yet pleasant, being very careful of the well being of others.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a sleeveless brown suit that includes yellow stripes, shorts, and an elongated collar. He also wears grey shoes with yellow soles.

Quirk

Ishiyama's Quirk, Cement, allows him to manipulate any cement-based material that he touches, allowing him to deform the object into various shapes and sizes, changing its appearance.

Fighting Style

Ishiyama's fighting style consists of Long-Ranged and Support.
