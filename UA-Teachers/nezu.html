Principal Nezu

Nezu, also known as Mr Principal, is a Pro Hero and the principal of UA High School. He is eccentric, polite, and persistent educator. He is not above putting himself on the front lines or in danger, with his focus being the safety of his students.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a white dress shirt, a dark red tie, black double-breasted waistcoat and matching dress trousers, and orange lace-up sneakers with increadily thick soles.

Quirk

Nezu's Quirk, High Specs, grants him a high level of intelligence that surpasses that of humans. This has enable him to live freely like a normal person, despite being an animal.

Fighting Style

Nezu's fighting style consists of Command and Support. He is an exceptional strategist who is able to execute complicated plans in a short manner of time due to his Quirk.
