Lunch Rush

Cook Hero: Lunch Rush, is a Pro Hero and a Chef at the cafeteria of U.A. High School.

Appearance

Lunch Rush wears a cooking apron and a chef hat as well as a mask that covers his entire face. He also wears white gloves and shoes.

Quirk

Lunch Ruch's Quirk is unknown.

Further Information

Lunch Rush was born in the Niigata Prefecture. Although he has not said a word, he seems to have an upbeat personality, as he always gives a thumbs-up as a form of reply.
