Denki Kaminari

Denki Kaminari, also known as Stun Gun Hero: Chargebolt, is a student in Class 1-A at UA High School. He has a friendly, social, and energetic personailty who likes to hang out with people. Though he is very academically bright and can become easily distraught during hero-work, he has shown to be confident when it counts.

Hero Costume

First Design

His hero costume consisted of a plain white shirt, under an open black jacket with a white lightning pattern, and matching trousers with two lines running down the legs. He also had a single, square-shaped earphone with a radio antenna over his right ear.

Second Design

For his Provisional Hero License Exam, he changes his costume to a slightly shorter black jacket with fewer white patterns and a yellow-rimmed lightning across each shoudler. He also has the same black trousers with small zig-zags near each cuffs, a white belt and white v-neck shirt. Around his right wrist, he wears a wristband with a circular mechanism from which he can shoot disc-shpaed Pointers which aids him in aiming with his Quirk. Addtionally, he wears a headset with a blue-tinted visor.

Quirk

Kaminari's Quirk, Electrification, allows him to generate electricity and covers his body in it.

The main drawback to his Quirk is that his brain can short-circuit when he exceeds his wattage limit, leaving him in a vulnerable state for 1 hour. In addition, when he emits electricity over a distance, he has little to no control over it. Though intense training, he increases the amount of electricity he can handle.

Fighting Style

Kaminari's fighting style consists of Melee and Support Combat. Using his Quirk, he gains a defense mechanism that shocks anyone who comes into contact with him, leaving him slightly, if not completely, paralysed. As a result, he can be fairly difficult individual to face in battle, especially those that rely on close-quarters combat.
