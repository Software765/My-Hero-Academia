Eijiro Kirishima

Eijiro Kirishima, also known as the Sturdy Hero: Red Riot, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Eijiro is muscular and of average height. He has short, spiky hair, which was naturally black before he dyed it red for U.A., and red eyes with a small scar just above his right eye. When entering U.A., he wears the standard male U.A. uniform, only instead of wearing the usual brown shoes, he wears dark red trainers.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of two dark red, gear-shaped shoulder pads, a jagged sash joining from his left pad to the right of his belt, which has the letter 'R' in the centre. He also wears baggy black trousers with a half cape and black boots. He leaves his chest bare and wears a wired face guard.

For the Provisional Hero License Exam, he upgrades his costume, now wearing black sleeves to prevent him hurting civilains with his Quirk during rescues.

Quirk

Eijiro's Quirk is called Hardening. It gives him the power to harden and sharpen any or all parts of his body, causing his flesh to become jagged and rock-like.

A drawback to his Quirk is that there is a limit to how much damage his hardened skin can withstand, causing it to slowly dissipate and make him lose stamina. Through intense training, he is able to increase his strength and durability.

Further Information

Eijiro was born in the Chiba Prefecture. He has a outgoing, kind-hearted personailty that allows him to make friends easily. He is a big fan of the Pro Hero, Crimson Riot, becoming an inspiration to be a hero himself, adopting a specific code that motivates him to live without regrets.

He loves all things manly and loves to eat meat.

Status in Class 1-A

  • 7th Youngest in the Class
  • Student/Seat No. 8
  • Ranked 2nd place in the U.A. Entrance Exam
  • Ranked 8th in the Quirk Apprehension Test
  • Ranked 15th in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked in Top 8 in the U.A. Sports Festival
  • He passed in the Provisional Hero License Exam, earning his license.
