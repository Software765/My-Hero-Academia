Principal Nezu

Nezu, also known as Mr Principal, is a Pro Hero and the Principal of U.A. High School. He is the extremely rare case of an animal who manifested a Quirk.

Appearance

Nezu has a short stature, who appears to be a possible combination of many animals, including a dog, mouse and bear. He has the head of a mouse with circular black eyes and a large scar over his right eye. He also has white fur, large dog-like paws and a thin cat-like tail.

Outfit

He wears a white dress shirt, a dark red tie, a black double-breasted waistcoat and matching dress trousers. He also wears orange lace-up sneakers, which are similar to the ones usually worn by Izuku Midoriya.

Quirk

Nezu's Quirk is called High Spec. It grants him a high level of intelligence that surpasses that of humans. This Quirk allows him to live among socitey, despite being an animal.

Further Information

Nezu was born in Tokyo. He has a eccentric, polite and persistent personality, all befitting to his role as the Principal.

He loves brushing and is also a smoker.
