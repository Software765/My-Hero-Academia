Hizashi Yamada

Hizashi Yamada, also known as Voice Hero: Present Mic, is a Pro Hero who teaches English at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Hizashi has a tall and slim figure. He has long, blonde hair spiked upwards, greenish-yellow eyes and a small mustache. He is almost always seen with a large smile on his face.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of a black jacket with a very tall collar, matching black trousers and knee-high boots. He also wears tan-coloured shoulder pads, a red belt, red elbow pads and black fingerless gloves. In addition, he wears a pair of orange-tinted shades and a directional speaker around his neck.

Quirk

Hizashi's Quirk is called Voice. It allows him to increase the volume of his voice, giving him an ability that is similar to a sonic scream.

A drawback to his Quirk is that his screams do not travel well underground.

Further Information

Hizashi was born in Tokyo. He has the eccentric persona of a radio host, maintaining a certain level of excitment or volume regardless of the situation.

He loves radio and TV and loves to eat fried chicken.
