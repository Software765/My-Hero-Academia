Nirengeki Shoda

Nirengeki Shoda, also known as Mines, is a student and the vice class representative of Class 1-B at UA High School. He has a very humble attitude, as he will not take credit for something he feels he didn't earn. During battle, he shows to be focused and good at teamwork.

Hero Cosutme

Shoda's hero costume consists of a black, high-collared suit with a trim of white pale lines around its collar, hem, torso and shoulders. He wears plain black trousers, white knee-high boots, and a blue scouter-looking device over his left eye. He also has a connected radar on the back of one of his gloved hands.

Quirk

Shoda's Quirk, Twin Impact, allows him to strike a target and then remotely make the point of contact expereince a second impact. The second imapct is several times stronger than the inital blow.

He can use this power to create a force of propulsion, such as to bestow an impact on an object to send it flying at opponents, or to send possible weapons away.

Fighting Style

Shoda's fighting style consists of Close-ranged Combat. He not only excels in one-to-one combat but also possesses good Quirk handling. His Quirk allows him to remotely cause attacks to repeat a second time, and with more force tha the initial impact. He can also exploit his Quirk for mobility purposes, such as jumping higher and dashing faster.

Shoda's known UA Data

  • Student/Seat No. 10
