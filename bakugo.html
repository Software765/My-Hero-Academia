Katsuki Bakugo

Katsuki Bakugo, also known as Kacchan by his childhood friends, is the deuteragonist of the My Hero Academia anime. He is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Katsuki is of average height with a silm, muscular build. He has short, spiky ash-blond hair and sharp, bright red eyes. When at U.A., he wear the offical uniform, with the top few buttons of his white shirt undone and his pants being loose, sagging at his ankles.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of a tight, black, sleeveless tank top with a orange 'X' in the middle and a metallic neck brace. He wears large grenade-like gauntlets, a belt that carries grenades, baggy pants and black and slightly orange, knee-high combat boots. He also wear a black, jagged eyemask, which has large, orange-rimmed flare shapes on each side.

In the winter, he wears a large, black jacket with a high collar, so that he can still sweat to combat the coldness.

Quirk

Katuski's Quirk is called Explosion. It allows him to screte nitroglycerin-like sweat from the palms of his hands and ignite it on command, creating explosions. The more he sweats, the stronger the explosions.

The drawback of his quirk is that when used exsessively, he can cause his muscules to become strained. Through intense training, he has increase the scale of his attacks

Further Information

Katsuki was born near the Shizuoka Prefecture and lived his mother, Mitsuki and father, Masaru Bakugo. He has a crude, arrogant and aggrestive personality, often coming across as an anti-hero, or even a potential villain to most people. But this attitude somewhat changes when he attends U.A., becoming less antagonistic. His inspiration into becoming a hero comes from All Might, wanting to always win like him.

His favourite activity is mountain climbing and he loves to eat spicy food.

Status in Class 1-A
