Kinoko Komori

Kinoko Komori, also known as Shemage, is a student in Class 1-B in UA High School. She may come across as shy, but she has an enthusiastic personality when it comes to using her Mushroom Quirk. She also possessed a kind side.

Hero Cosutme

Komori's hero costume consists of a red and white pattern turtleneck dress with long sleeves and furred cuffs, with a matching mushroom cap-shaped hat. She wears pale pink knee-high boots, frilled shafts in the same pattern and colour as her dress, and a dark brown belt with a pair of gun-like spray bottles. Altogether, her costume resembles a mushroom.

Quirk

Komori's Quirk, Mushroom, allows her to spread various types of fungus spores from her body. The spores will quickly grow into full-grown mushrooms once they make contact with a soild surface. They only last for 2-3 hours and spread better in a humid environment.

Fighting Style

Komori's fighting style consists of Long Ranged Support. She is able to produce spores that can generate hordes of mushrooms in various, unexpected areas to surprise and overwhelm her opponents, as well as provide a smokescreen to simultaneously obscure her opponents' line of view and conceal the presense of both herself and her allies.
