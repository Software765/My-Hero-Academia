Jurota Shishida

Jurota Shishida, also known as Gevaudan, is a student in Class 1-B at UA High School. He is quite an intellectual student as he uses a fairly verbose way of speaking. While transformed, however, he can be rather boisterous and energetic.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a regular pair of pants, a shirt collar with no shirt and a pair of stylised goggles in place of his normal glasses.

Quirk

Shishida's Quirk, Beast, allows him to transform into a large, monstrous beast-man. This form drastically augments his strength, speed, and durability, as well as his sense of hearing and smell.

The main drawback of his Quirk is that he becomes more wild, making him reckless.

Fighting Style

Shishida's fighting style consists of Melee Combat. With his Quirk, he becomes so powerful, it becomes diffcult to defeat him in a head-to-head battle as his Quirk grants him an increase in his physical prowess. In addition, he can run on all fours and attacks his opponents by mauling them.
