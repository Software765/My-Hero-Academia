Tetsutetsu Tetsutetsu

Tetsutetsu Tetsutetsu, also known as Real Steel, is a student in Class 1-B at UA High School. He has a very hot-headed and outspoken personality, being very vocal about his intention and has a one-track mind. Despite his stubbornness, he shows to deeply care about his peers.

Hero Cosutme

Tetsutetsu's hero costume consists of a dark green jumpsuit, cut off just below his chest. He wears black boots with metal soles, heels and front, steel straps over his shoulders that is connected with a red oval in the center, two thinner bands around his biceps, and a small plate on either side of his waist. Additionally, he wears a metal jaw-guard around his face, with the letters "Fe" stamped on a plate on each side of his face.

Quirk

Tetsutetsu's Quirk, Steel, allows him to turn his skin into steel. It both augmented defensive abilites to withstand attacks, as well as the augmented strength of his unarmed physical attacks.

The main drawback of his Quirk is he will from iron fatigue if he overuses, resulting in his metal form losing its durability. It is implied that his Quirk's strength is fueled and affected by his diet containing iron.

Fighting Style

Tetsutetsu's fighting style consists of Melee Combat. Thanks to his Quirk, his fighting style is straightforward and reckless, letting his hardened body take opposing hits while delivering counterattacks. He is also capable of turning himself into a human shield for allies or defenseles civilians.
