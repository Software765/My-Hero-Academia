Tenya Iida

Tenya Iida, also known as Ingenium, is the class representative of Class 1-A at U.A. High School and is one of the main protagonists of the My Hero Academia anime.

Appearance

Tenya is relatively tall and muscular with a wide frame. He has short dark blue hair, with a small patch completely shaved near the base of his head. He also has dark blue eyes and wear rectangular lenses. His calves are very thick, with three silver exhaust pipes protruding from each one.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of a black one-piece suit with a high collar, over which he wears various pieces of armor, including a chest plate, a metal collar around his neck and upper arm, and metal vambraces that extend past his elbows. He also wear a helment that covers his entire face and silver knee-high boots.

Quirk

Tenya's Quirk is called Engine. He has car-like engines in each of his calves, giving him super speed and extreme kicking power. Through intense training, he is able to increase his leg strength and stamina, so he can run faster for longer periods of time.

Further Information

Tenya was born in Tokyo and lived with his mother and father. He has a older brother, Tensai Iida, who was the Pro Hero Ingenium before he had to retire due to a serious injury sustained by his encounter with the Hero Killer, Stain.

He tends to take things seriously and seems to be severe and unfriendly, but is actually earnest and quite intelligent.

He loves studying and loves to eat beef stew.

Status in Class 1-A

  • 10th Youngest in the Class
  • Student/Seat No. 4
  • Ranked 6th place in the U.A. Entrance Exam
  • Ranked 4th in the Quirk Apprehension Test
  • Ranked 2nd in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked 4th in the U.A. Sports Festival
  • Scored 80 in the Provisional Hero License Exam, earning his license.
