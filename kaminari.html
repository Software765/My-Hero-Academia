Denki Kaminari

Denki Kaminari, also known as Stun Gun Hero: Chargebolt, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Denki is a little skinner than most of the other male students in his class, not having much visible muscle mass. He has relatively short blonde hair, with a lightning-shaped streak on the left of his side fringe and yellow eyes. When going to U.A., he wears the offical U.A. unfiorm.

Hero Costume

For his original costume, he wears a plain white shirt, an open black jacket with white lighning pattern and matching trousers with two lines running down the legs. He has a single, square-shaped earphone over his right ear.

For his Provisional Hero License Exam, he changes his costume to a slightly shorter black jacket with fewer white patterns and a yellow-rimmed lighning across each shoudler. He also wears black trousers with small zig=zags near each cuffs, a white belt and white v-neck shirt. Around his right wrist, he wears a wristband with a circular mechanism from which he can shoot disc-shpaed Pointers which aids him in aiming with his Quirk. Addtionally, he wears a headset with a blue-tinted visor.

Quirk

Denki's Quirk is called Electrification. It allows him to generate electricity and covers his body with it. One drawback to his quirk is that when he emits this energy over a distance, he has little to no control over it.

Another drawback is that his brain can short-circuit when he exceeds his wattage limit, leaving him in a vulnerable state for 1 hour. Though intense training, he increases the amount of electricity he can handle.

Further Information

Denki was born in the Saitama Prefecture. He has a friendly, social and energetic personality, which allows him to interacting with others easily, even Katsuki Bakugo.

He loves to eat hamburgers and anything that's currently trending.
