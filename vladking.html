Sekijiro Kan

Sekijiro Kan, also known as the Blood Hero: Vlad King, is a Pro Hero and the homeroom teacher of U.A. High School's Class 1-B.

Appearance

Sekijiro is very tall and wide-set with a bulky and muscular build. He has short pale grey hair spiked upwards, small black eyes and a few visible on the left side of his face and neck. He also has a wide mouth with two prominent lower canines.

Outfit

His hero costume consists of a dark red skin-tight suit with thick black trims. It has a very low u-shaped collar that exposes the middle of his pectoral muscules. He also wears white, over-the-knee boots, thick silver vambraces, red gloves, metallic belt and an orange eyemask. There is a thin wire that connects from the back of his neck to his left vanbrance.

Quirk

Sekijiro's Quirk is called Blood Control. It allows him to freely manipulate his own blood after it has left his body. He can also harden the ejected blood, allowing him to trap his opponent. It appears that the large levels of blood loss he would use is unaffected to him.

Further Information

Sekijiro was born in the Aomori Prefecture. He has a caring personality, especially to his students. He is willing to go out of his way to protect them and encourages them.

He loves dog, owning one himself.
