Ectoplasm

Ectoplasm is a Pro Hero and member of the UA High School faculty who teaches mathematics. He is steadfast and unrelenting in his craft. He desires the best from his students and isn't afraid to push them.

Hero Cosutme

His hero costume consists of a skin-tight black bodysuit with a golden striped neck area, under a tan cloak with a high collar that covers most of his body. He also wears a matching black helmet that covers his entire head, baggy dark grey trousers, and a pair of thin, lightwear prosthetic legs.

Quirk

Ectoplasm's Quirk, Clones, gives him the ability to produce streams of ectoplasm from his mouth and transform it into clones of himself. He can create as many as 30 clones at once.

Fighting Style

Ectoplasm's fighting style is Melee Combat. He excels in flurries of fast kicks in close range combat despite not having his legs anymore.
