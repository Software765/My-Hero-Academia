Higari Maijima

Higari Maijima, also known as Excavation Hero: Power Loader, is a Pro Hero and faculty memeber at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Higari has a short and skinny build, with a very youthful appearance for his advanced age. He has spiky ginger hair, dull blue eyes and a notably large mouth. His hands appear to be the largest part of his body, with an orange L-shaped piece at the end of each finger.

Outfit

His hero costume consists of a large yellow helmet in the shape of a excavator claw. He leaves himself shirtless, wears a pair of large, grey fingerless gloves with metal guards and a pair of plain cyan trousers.

Quirk

Higari's Quirk is called Iron Claws. His fingers are tipped with metallic claws that are good for digging through the ground.

Further Information

Higari was born in the Osaka Prefecture. He has a strict personality, who desires to see his students excel with constraint.

He loves cars.
