Mashirao Ojiro

Mashirao Ojiro, also known as the Martial Arts Hero: Tailman, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Mashirao has a muscular build. He has short blonde hair, thin black eyes and a thick, long tail with a hairy tip. When attending U.A., he wears the standard male U.A. uniform, only instead of wearing the brown dress shoes, he wears pale grey sneakers.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of a plain karate gi, tied with a black belt and its collar partially lined with fur.

For the Provisional License Exam, he upgrades by adding navy blue fingerless gloves.

Quirk

Mashirao's Quirk is called Tail. He has a long, powerful, strudy tail, which he is incredibly adept at using. Through intense training, he increases the strength and durability of his tail.

Further Information

Mashirao was born in Tokyo. He has a very calm and noble personality, with a deep sense of dignity. He also described as a hard worker.

He loves doing martial arts. He may also like watching martial arts films.

Status in Class 1-A

  • 2nd Oldest in the Class
  • Student/Seat No. 6
  • His rank in the U.A. Entrance Exam is unknown.
  • Ranked 7th in the Quirk Apprehension Test
  • Ranked 8th in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked in the Top 42 in the U.A. Sports Festival
  • Scored 61 in the Provisional Hero License Exam, earning his license.
