Itsuka Kendo

Itsuka Kendo, also known as Battle Fist, is a student and the class representative of Class 1-B at UA High School. She is a spirited person who is thoughtful to others. Overall, she is friendly and shows sympathy to others outside her class.

Hero Cosutme

Kendo's hero costume consists of a turquoise knee-length qipao, over which she has a black double-breasted corset, ending just below her breasts. She wears black short pants under the qipao, white-heeled navy blue boots with wide vamps, a thin black domino mask around her eyes, and a loose brown utility belt around her hips with a satchel attached.

Winter Costume

Her winter costume switches out her normal qipoa for one with long wrist-length sleeves.

Quirk

Kendo's Quirk, Big Fist, grants her the ability to enlarge both of her hands to a gigantic size. With the increased size comes enhanced striking power and gripping capabilites.

Fighting Style

Kendo's fighting style consists of Melee Combat. With her Quirk, she has enough power to break through most defenses. The sudden expansion of her hands can be used to surprise foes, or to increase the power of her attacks through the momentum of the expansion. Her enlarged hands is also useful for transporting others and restraining opponents.
