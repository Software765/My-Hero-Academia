Mezo Shoji

Mezo Shoji, also known as the Tentacle Hero: Tentacole, is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School.

Appearance

Mezo has a tall and muscular build. He has plae grey hair that covers most of his face and black eyes. He has six arms, all attached to each other by a web of skin. Most of his face is hidden by a blue mask. When attending U.A., he wears the standard male U.A. uniform. The only difference is that his blazer is sleeveless to make room for his six tentacles and he wears thick-soled black espadrilles instead of the usual brown dress shoes.

Hero Costume

His hero costume consists of a tight blue tank top with six white markings that resembles eyes arranged like a dice. This is connected to a darker, more indigo-coloured mask. He also wears a belt, slightly baggy, blue trousers and indigo boots.

Quirk

Mezo's Quirk is called Dupli-Arms. He has a set od tentacles alongside his arms, where he can transform the tips of these tentacles to replicate other parts of his body, such as mouth, ears and eyes.

Further Information

Mezo was born in the Fukuoka Prefecture. Despite his apperance, he has a very friendly and gentle personality, who works nicely with others and doesn't hold grudges.

He loves to eat takoyaki and squid ink pasta.

Status in Class 1-A

  • The Youngest in the Class
  • Student/Seat No. 11
  • His rank in the U.A. Entrance Exam is unknown
  • Ranked 6th in the Quirk Apprehension Test
  • Ranked 10th in the Mid-Term Exams
  • Ranked in the Top 42 in the U.A. Sports Festival
  • He passed in the Provisional Hero License Exam, earning his license.
